What's the difference between OPT and H1B visa sponsorship?

+

OPT (Optional Practical Training) is a 12-month work authorization for F-1 students, with a 24-month STEM extension available. H1B is a 3-year work visa that requires employer sponsorship and is subject to annual caps. Many companies sponsor both - OPT for recent graduates and H1B for longer-term employment. Our platform shows which visa types each company supports.
